DropSwift — SaaS Landing Page + Ad Campaign

Role: Campaign Designer + Landing Page Builder Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Canva, Leonardo.ai
Live Demo

Project Goal

Design a landing page and ad creative package to drive trial sign-ups for a SaaS product.

Target Audience

eCommerce store owners looking to automate operations.

Tools Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Canva, Leonardo.ai

My Contribution

Created campaign messaging, landing page flow, and ad creative to maintain a consistent paid acquisition journey.

Outcome

Landing page and ad assets aligned for cold and retargeting campaigns to improve click-to-signup rates.

Marketing Goal & Funnel Stage

Top-of-funnel awareness and mid-funnel consideration for SaaS buyers, using paid social creative to move users from curiosity to demo interest.

The ad variants were designed for different funnel stages: cold traffic sees bold awareness messaging, while warmer audiences get product-benefit copy highlighting quick setup. Palette and CTA choices were meant to signal professional automation for ecommerce owners and reduce friction toward a trial signup. I used AIDA-style sequencing to capture attention, build interest with pain-point relief, and drive action with a clear demo request CTA.

Result / What I'd Measure

Target metric: demo request rate. Estimated based on SaaS landing page benchmarks and creative variation tests for paid social.
